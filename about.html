Our Story

Founded in 2010, Hadi Estate began as a small family-run real estate business with a vision to transform the property market through integrity, innovation, and exceptional service. What started as a modest operation with just three agents has grown into one of the most respected real estate firms in the region.

Our founder, Hadi Ahmed, recognized early on that the real estate industry needed a fresh approach—one that prioritized clients' needs above all else. This client-first philosophy remains at the core of everything we do today.

Over the past decade, we've helped thousands of families find their dream homes, assisted investors in building profitable portfolios, and supported developers in bringing visionary projects to life. Our success is measured not just in transactions closed, but in the lasting relationships we've built along the way.
